About Us

Sasi Institute of Technology & Engineering Sasi Institute of Technology & Engineering started its functioning with four academic programs like CSE, ECE, EEE & ME. Now the institute offers twelve UG programs (AIML, CE, CSE, CST, CSE(AIML), CSE(DS), CSE(IoT&CS), ECE, ECT, EEE & ME), six PG Programs ((CSE, VLSI&ES, CE&SP, PE, CAD/CAM, MBA) and Three Diploma courses (DECE, DEEE, DME). It has created a sensation in the field of engineering education in a very short span of time and accredited by NBA & NAAC with ‘A’r̥ grade, recognised by UGC under section 2(f) & 12(b), approved by AICTE New Delhi, permanently affiliated to JNTUK.
